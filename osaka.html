Osaka

Osaka is a large port city and commercial center on the Japanese island of Honshu. It's known for its modern architecture, nightlife and hearty street food.

Things to Do/Places to visit in Osaka

  1. Universal Studios Japan

    2. Avoid the crowds waiting to purchase tickets for Universal Studios Japan with your pre-booked 1-day Universal Express® Pass for this popular theme park in Osaka. Enjoy attractions such as Hollywood District and Jurassic Park; and rides such as Despicable Me Minion Mayhem and The Wizarding World of Harry Potter. Perfect for a family-friendly excursion in Osaka.

  2. Dotonbori

    3. The heart of Osaka nightlife, this area is filled with theaters, restaurants, cafes, bars, arcades and nightclubs.

  3. Osaka Aquarium Kaiyukan

    4. See the wide range of habitats that make up the Pacific Rim on a tour of this fascinating aquarium, which takes you from one environment to another and lets you feel as though you’re underwater.
